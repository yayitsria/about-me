All about friendship bracelets!

Page 1

I've been making friendship bracelets for about 6 years now. While it isn't that hard to start doing, it definitely requires a lot of patience and an eye for detail. Especially for bigger projects, there are hundreds to potentially thousands of knots required to to create a bracelet.

You're not limited to bracelets, either. Friendship bracelet patterns can also make excellent bookmarks and necklaces as well!

The only required materials are:

  • Embroidery floss
  • Scissors

    • My favorite website to get patterns from is friendship-bracelets.net.
